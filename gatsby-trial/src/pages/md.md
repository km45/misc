### inline math

### display math

$$
\begin{aligned}
\cos{z} &= \frac{ e^{iz} + e^{-iz} }{ 2  }, \\
\sin{z} &= \frac{ e^{iz} - e^{-iz} }{ 2i }
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{ \text{d} }{ \text{d}z } \cos{z} &= - \sin{z}, \\
\frac{ \text{d} }{ \text{d}z } \sin{z} &=   \cos{z}
\end{aligned}
$$
